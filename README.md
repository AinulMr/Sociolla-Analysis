# Sociolla Analysis
Sociolla is an e-commerce platform based in Indonesia, particularly known in the beauty and cosmetics industry. Sociolla provides a variety of beauty products, including makeup, skincare, hair care products, and other beauty items. Customers can browse their online catalog and make purchases through Sociolla's website or mobile app. Aside from being an online store, Sociolla is also known for providing beauty product information and reviews, providing users with beauty guides and tips, and engaging in promotions and collaborations with well-known brands in the beauty industry.
# Business Problem
Does the rating of a product influence the number of repeat purchases?
# Objectives
Need to perform Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) and ultimately produce insights and business recommendation. Perform Simple Linear Regression between rating product and repurchase count.
